BLACK AND WHITE SUMMER

A street photography project that captures 100 days in New York in the summer of 2016. With its deep history, varied culture, busy streets and dynamic people, these streets glimmer with endless emotional stories. All the content for “Black and white summer” is produced by Edna Pei-Yu Lee.
